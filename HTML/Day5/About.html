Tamil Nadu Election 2026 – Election Process & Key Features

The Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Election 2026 is one of the most significant political events in the state because it reflects both traditional Dravidian politics and emerging new political forces.

Election System,Voting Process

Tamil Nadu follows a First-Past-The-Post (FPTP) system. Total constituencies: 234 A candidate only needs to win more votes than others in a constituency (not majority votes overall). Conducted under the supervision of the Election Commission of India (ECI). Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs) and VVPAT used for transparency. Strict security arrangements in sensitive areas.
